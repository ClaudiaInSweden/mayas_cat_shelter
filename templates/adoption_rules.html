Adoption Process

Things to consider before adopting a cat

To adopt a cat from a cat shelter involves a lot of responsibility. Cats who come to our cat shelter have often been homeless for some reasons, or need a new home due to illness or death of their previous owners. We don't want our cats to go through such a traumatic experience again so we are very carefully in chosing a new home.

Questions you need to ask yourself are:

  • Does your financial situation allow for unexpected expenditures, like acute veterinary care which can be expensive even with an insurance?
  • Can you afford high-quality food, insurance and litter even in a couple of years?
  • Do you have enough time for a cat?
  • Do you have somebody who can take care of the cat if you are sick or when you travel?
